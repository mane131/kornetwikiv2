OVERVIEW

Kornet is an old Roblox revival aiming to recreate the authentic feel of classic Roblox gameplay and community interaction. We preserve legacy styles, mechanics, and aesthetics from earlier versions of Roblox (circa 2016). Founded on November 7, 2025, we're bringing back the nostalgia.

The main hub for the community is our Discord server, where members interact, get updates, and participate in revival-related events just like the old days.

Official Discord Server: https://discord.gg/u5En32wvwM

WHAT IS AN OLD ROBLOX REVIVAL?

An old Roblox revival is a community project that aims to bring back the nostalgic experience of playing Roblox from its earlier years (roughly 2006-2016). These revivals often feature the older blocky graphics, simple UIs, classic games, and that authentic community feel that modern Roblox has lost.

RULES & GUIDELINES

To maintain the classic Roblox community feel, we follow these rules:

  • Be respectful to all members (just like old Roblox)
  • No harassment, hate speech, or toxicity
  • No inappropriate content or slurs
  • Follow staff instructions
  • No advertising without permission
  • Keep it family-friendly like classic Roblox
